               <label class="btnLabel">What is it</label>
               <button class="copyBtn">
Cash26 is a service that lets you withdraw or deposit cash at retail stores using your smartphone. Simply open the Cash26 map in your N26 app to find the nearest participating store. 
Generate a Cash26 barcode from your app and show it to the cashier to make your deposit or withdrawal at the counter.

This convenient service eliminates the need for bank or ATM visits, paperwork or lengthy processes. 
Cash26 offers a simple and efficient way to conduct financial transactions at participating retail stores with ease.
             </div>

              <div class="pair">
               <label class="btnLabel">Withdraw</label>
               <button class="copyBtn">
-Go to the Cardstab in your N26 app,
-then tap Cash26> Withdrawal and
-choose the amount you want to withdraw.
-Next, enter your confirmation PIN(new tab),
-and confirm your transaction on the pop-up screen.
-Your N26 app will generate a barcode to show the cashier, who will scan it and hand over your cash.
-Kindly note that you can withdraw up to €200 each time, with a maximum withdrawal limit of €900 within 24 hours. 
             </div>

              <div class="pair">
               <label class="btnLabel">Deposit</label>
               <button class="copyBtn">
-Open your app and go to the Cards tab,
-then tap Cash26> Deposit.
-Next, choose the amount you want to deposit, with a minimum of €50.
-Enter your confirmation PIN(new tab), and
-confirm your transaction on the pop-up screen.
-Once you’re ready, show your Cash26 barcode to the cashier to scan, and hand them the cash.
-Your Cash26 deposit will be credited to your N26 account within 10 minutes. 
             </div>

              <div class="pair">
               <label class="btnLabel">Also note</label>
               <button class="copyBtn">
Kindly also note that:

-You can deposit cash at the cashier of any Cash26 partner store. Keep in mind that your barcode will only be valid for 10 minutes. 
-After that, your barcode will expire and you'll have to generate a new one.
-The money should appear instantly in the account
-The minimum CASH26 deposit is €50 per transaction, and you can deposit up to €999 in a 24-hour period.
-Regardless of your deposit amount, you’ll be charged a transaction fee of 1.5% on the total amount.
-There also is a combined limit for withdrawals and deposits of 999€ per 24 hour.

               <label class="btnLabel">What spaces are</label>
               <button class="copyBtn">
Spaces is an innovative financial organization tool that enables users to create up to 10 customizable subaccounts for managing their finances more effectively.
With features like instant money transfers, savings goal setting, access control through locking/unlocking, balance hiding options, and balance statement downloads, 
Spaces offers a user-friendly and versatile solution for individuals to better organize, track, and control their financial resources and goals.
               </button></button>
             </div>

              <div class="pair">
               <label class="btnLabel">IBAN for spaces</label>
               <button class="copyBtn">
You can now assign IBANs to your Spaces! You can create up to 10 Spaces with IBANs in total. Spaces with IBANs enable you to:

Make SEPA transfers directly from your Space
Use your Space for direct debits
Receive SEPA transfers to your Space
Note: Incoming SEPA transfers must always be addressed to your name, not the name of the Space.
               </button></button>

-Alternatively, you can link your card through the Cards tabs:

-In the home screen, tap Cards
-Tap Card Settings
-Tap Linked to
-Select your preferred Space and tap Link to >card name<
